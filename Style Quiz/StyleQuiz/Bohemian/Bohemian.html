Bohemian

Bohemian style is characterised with artistic interests like artsy style. However, the difference is that Bohemian style focuses on some exotic patterns and textures. The free-spirited Bohemian draws inspirations from the gypsy look with their intricate patterns, pleasant blouses, multiple chains, head scarves, and also hippy ponchos and the dye jeans. Bohemian is well known fo rfeatures that are considered non-traditional in appearance.
